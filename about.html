About Me

Hi there, I'm Ashley.

Hi there, I'm Ashley (she/her), a UI/UX Designer at Locate Alpha, focused on how people make high-stakes consumer decisions in proptech. I studied Human-Centered Design, Entrepreneurship, Classics, and Studio Art at Dartmouth College.

Recently, I worked on the Lennar Investor Marketplace which has gained 6,500+ users and driven $64M+ in new revenue for Lennar within the first two months.

I've recently been experimenting with ways to incorporate AI into my workflow as both a tool and fellow collaborator. I've found it particularly helpful for maintaining design system components (especially for discrepancies between Figma and code), adding more iterations to my brainstorming phases, and quickly creating interactive prototypes for early user testing.

Outside of work, I dance bachata, draw figure models, and watch birds.
